Top Questions

What is Billie Eilish's real name?
Billie Eilish's full name is Billie Eilish Pirate Baird O’Connell.
When was Billie Eilish born?
Billie Eilish was born on December 18, 2001, in Los Angeles, California.
Did Billie Eilish attend school?
Billie Eilish’s parents were inspired by Hanson, a ’90s pop band formed by a group of homeschooled brothers from Tulsa, Oklahoma, to teach Billie and her older brother, Finneas, at home and to encourage them to explore passions outside of the traditional education system.
